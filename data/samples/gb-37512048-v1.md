Monday
------


From 2\.30pm


### Questions to government


<ul><li>health-related benefits assessments being conducted in person</li><li>shortage of affordable housing for young people</li><li>assessment of the performance of Royal Mail</li><li>'Selective Inclusion' report by the Sutton Trust.</li></ul>


### Legislation


The Lords will debate the main purpose and key themes of the [Financial Services and Markets Bill](https://www.parliament.uk/business/news/2026/june-2026/financial-services-and-markets-bill-begins-journey-through-lords/).


### Debates


The House will debate the following committee reports:


<ul><li><a href="https://publications.parliament.uk/pa/ld5901/ldselect/pubserv/181/18102.htm">Reforming the Child Maintenance Service</a> (Public Services Committee)</li><li><a href="https://publications.parliament.uk/pa/ld5901/ldselect/ldindreg/225/22502.htm">The Building Safety Regulator, Building a Better Regulator</a> (Industry and Regulators Committee).</li></ul>


Tuesday
-------


From 2\.30pm


### Questions to government


<ul><li>supporting and funding the work of the British Council</li><li>use of electric bikes and scooters on roads</li><li>impact on further education colleges of their inability to recover VAT on expenditure</li><li>fourth question to be determined.</li></ul>


### Legislation


The Lords will debate the main purpose and key themes of the [Commercial Payments Bill](https://www.parliament.uk/business/news/2026/june-2026/commercial-payments-bill-house-of-lords-second-reading/).


### Regulations


Members will debate [clean air zones central services](https://statutoryinstruments.parliament.uk/instrument/hFGZOjhW) regulations and a [motion to regret](https://lordsbusiness.parliament.uk/ItemOfBusiness?itemOfBusinessId=171465&sectionId=38&businessPaperDate=2026-06-09) the regulations from [Lord Moylan](https://members.parliament.uk/member/4883/contact).


Wednesday
---------


From 3pm


### Questions to government


<ul><li>publication of the impact assessment of the 56-day move-on period pilot for those granted refugee status</li><li>Best Start Family Hubs</li><li>priorities for farming and food production</li><li>fourth question to be determined.</li></ul>


### Debates


The House will debate the following committee reports:


<ul><li><a href="https://publications.parliament.uk/pa/ld5901/ldselect/ldautismact/205/20502.htm">Time to deliver: the Autism Act 2009 and the new autism strategy</a> (Autism Act 2009 Committee)</li><li><a href="https://publications.parliament.uk/pa/ld5901/ldselect/ldhomework/196/19602.htm">Is working from home working?</a> (Home-Based Working Committee)</li><li><a href="https://publications.parliament.uk/pa/ld5901/ldselect/ldbuiltenv/183/18302.htm">New Towns: Laying the Foundations</a> (Built Environment Committee).</li></ul>


Thursday

From 11am


### Questions to government


<ul><li>National Planning Policy Framework changes about sustainable drainage systems in new developments</li><li>legal framework for the proscription of the Iranian Revolutionary Guard Corps</li><li>tackling antisemitism in the UK</li><li>fourth question to be determined.</li></ul>
